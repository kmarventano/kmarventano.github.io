About Me

I'm a Software Engineer and graduate of NYU class of 2017 in the College of Arts and Science majoring in Computer Science and minoring in Mathematics. Prior to college, I never used a computer for anything more than Netflix and writing papers. I started out in college as a math major with aspirations to go into data analysis. My degree program required a few computer science classes. I was terrified of these classes, but I ended up really enjoying them, so much so that the second semester of my junior year, I switched to being a CS major.

After I graduated in December of 2017, I got a job as a Software Engineer at Google. I've worn many hats while working for Google, but my most recent work has been as a Fullstack Engineer in Local Search.
